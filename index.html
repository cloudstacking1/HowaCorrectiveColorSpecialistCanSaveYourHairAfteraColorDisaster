What is a Corrective Color Specialist?

A Corrective Color Specialist is a licensed hair professional trained specifically in color theory and advanced hair coloring techniques. Unlike regular stylists, these specialists focus on correcting previous hair color jobs whether its blotchy roots, uneven tones, brassy highlights, or oversaturated dark dye.

They dont just apply color they analyze your hairs condition, assess previous coloring history, and create a personalized strategy to achieve the perfect shade while keeping your hair healthy.
